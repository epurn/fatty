# Product Goal

Build Fatty as a serious, iOS-first calorie and macro tracker that removes the friction from logging food and exercise.

The app should:

- accept natural language food and exercise input,
- estimate with evidence rather than guesses,
- use deterministic math for calories, macros, targets, and exercise burn,
- show one editable result with source/status icons,
- learn from corrections and saved foods,
- protect personal body, food, and goal data by default,
- remain self-hostable through Docker Compose.

The app should not:

- moralize food,
- make medical claims,
- pretend vague inputs are precise,
- store data without product need,
- expose open-ended agent powers to untrusted content.
